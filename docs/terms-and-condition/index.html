Terms & Conditions

Welcome to the AjraSakha Hackathon. By participating, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

1. Eligibility​

The hackathon is open to students, professionals, and independent developers. Each team must consist of exactly four (4) members:

  • 1 Product Manager
  • 1 Designer
  • 2 Developers

2. Intellectual Property​

  • Teams retain ownership of their projects.
  • AjraSakha and Annam.AI reserve the right to showcase submissions for promotional or educational purposes.

3. Original Work​

All submissions must be original.
Plagiarism, copied codebases, or previously submitted projects will result in disqualification.

4. Code of Conduct​

Participants must:

  • Respect fellow participants and organizers
  • Avoid harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior
  • Maintain professionalism during all interactions

Violation may lead to immediate removal from the event.

5. Submission Rules​

  • Projects must be submitted before the deadline.
  • Late submissions will not be evaluated.
  • All required fields in the submission form must be completed.

6. Evaluation & Decisions​

Judges’ decisions are final and binding.
Projects will be evaluated based on innovation, usability, technical quality, and relevance to the problem statement.

7. Liability​

The organizers are not responsible for:

  • Data loss
  • Service outages
  • Personal device or internet issues

8. Acceptance​

By registering and participating, you agree to all the above terms.
