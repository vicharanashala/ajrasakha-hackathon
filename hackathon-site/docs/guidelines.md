# Guidelines

These guidelines ensure a fair, productive, and enjoyable experience for all participants of the **AjraSakha Hackathon**.

Please read them carefully before participating.

---

## 1. Team Formation

Each team must consist of **exactly four (4) members**:

- 1 Product Manager  
- 1 Designer  
- 2 Developers  

All team members must be registered on the AjraSakha platform.

---

## 2. Project Scope

Your project must align with the official hackathon theme:

> **Build an education platform that connects students and educators directly, enabling affordable, personalized, and accessible learning experiences.**

Projects that deviate significantly from this objective may not be evaluated.

---

## 3. Development Rules

- All work must be done **during the hackathon period**.
- You may use open-source libraries, frameworks, and APIs.
- Pre-built templates are allowed, but **the core logic and features must be developed by your team**.

---

## 4. Code Quality & Best Practices

Your submission should demonstrate:
- Clean, readable, and maintainable code
- Proper project structure
- Meaningful commit history (preferred)
- Basic error handling and validation

---

## 5. Design & User Experience

Your product should be:
- **Mobile-first**
- **Accessible**
- Easy to understand and navigate

A clean, user-friendly interface is as important as functionality.

---

## 6. Required Features

Your solution must include:

- User registration for students and educators  
- Course creation and course browsing  
- Basic user profiles  
- A minimal trust system (rating, review, or verification)

Optional but encouraged:
- Payments or contract system  
- Personalized learning paths  
- AI-powered recommendations  

---

## 7. Submission Guidelines

Each team must submit:

- A working demo (live link or local setup)
- Source code repository (GitHub preferred)
- A short description of:
  - What you built
  - How it works
  - The problem it solves

---

## 8. Judging Criteria

Projects will be evaluated based on:

- **Innovation**
- **Usability**
- **Technical execution**
- **Relevance to the problem statement**
- **Overall product vision**

---

## 9. Fair Play

Plagiarism, copied projects, or unethical behavior will result in immediate disqualification.

---

## 10. Support

If you face any issues during the hackathon, reach out to the AjraSakha support team or organizers.
